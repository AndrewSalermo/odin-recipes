Firecracker Shrimp

Sweet but tangy shrimp with flavor thats out of this world.

Perfect dish to make when you are craving seafood and want something crunchy but slightly sweet. Perfect as an appetizer or as a dinner dish.

  • 2 pounds of shrimp
  • 1 cup of panko
  • Sweet Chili sauce
  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 Eggs
  1. Wash and devein shrimp
  2. Prepare egg batter
  3. Prepare panko crumbs
  4. Heat oil to high
  5. Dip shrimp in egg batter then into panko crumbs
  6. Fry in oil for 6 minutes
  7. Take out and cover with chili oil to taste
