Lomo Saltado

Lomo Saltado Peruvian Dish

This dish is one of the most famous foods that Peru is known for. This dish involves a delicous combiantion of savory but salty flavors.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Onions
  • Rice
  • Fries
  • Tomatos
  1. Wash rice and cook in rice cooker
  2. Season steak with salt,pepper,garlic power, paprika
  3. Heat up oil in pan and begin sauteing onion and tomatos
  4. Add steak once vegetables are slightly cooked and add soy sauce and oyster sauce as needed
  5. Cut potatos and fry in oil
  6. Lastly plate and enjoy
