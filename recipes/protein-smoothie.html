Protein Smoothie

Vanilla Protein Smoothie with berries

Sweet and delicious protein smoothie thats perfect for morning breakfast or after the gym. Balanced nutrition of both protein and fruits for your diet.

  • 2 scoops of your favorite Protein powder
  • 1/2 cup of strawberries
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 1/2 cup of raspberries
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  1. Pour milk in blender
  2. Add protein powder of your choice
  3. Add fruit
  4. blend
  5. Once blended add in honey
  6. blend and enjoy!
